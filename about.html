About Us

Welcome to Translinkasia.com, the India's First transport website powered by most advanced computerized load matching systems. Translinkasia.com is here to keep trucks loaded, freights moving and India's trucking/lorry industry chugging along at a happy, healthy pace. We're committed to providing instant load board access to Lorry owners, truckers and Lorry brokers with cutting-edge technology that's user-friendly, economical, fast, and secure.

Translinkasia.com is the country's fastest growing truck load network. We are the most active freight exchange site in the India, saving our customers millions of wasted miles and unnecessary fuel emissions for the environment.

This easy-to-use site offers customers, the chance to search for trucks for their loads as per their requirement. Customers can book their desired no. of trucks to match their requirement. A customer can calculate the distance between their startup and destination place for their loads. Customer can also find the estimated cost for their loads.

Customers not only benefit from reduced empty running but save fuel by not having to travel far to collect their next load, returning to base in quicker time too. Our online, India-wide network makes customer contact quick and effective, without the need for endless phone calls. Customers are also rated by each other through our advanced star rating system, which allows feedback to be left by both parties, encouraging a reliable and high quality customer service.

Our Mission

Our mission over the coming years is to become the largest portal for the transport sector. We hope to allow all logistics businesses to work more closely, effectively and efficiently, using Translinkasia.com as their trusted Indian transport marketplace.
